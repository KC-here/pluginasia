Our Philosophy

At PluginAsia, we hold a core belief: the right talent strategy is the primary driver of both organizational success and personal fulfillment. In a world of constant disruption, businesses thrive or fail based on the quality of their leadership and the resilience of their people. Our mission is to serve as the strategic partner for organizations and individuals committed to navigating this complexity and achieving extraordinary outcomes.

The Strategic Edge: Oxford & Corporate Leadership

The PluginAsia methodology is rooted in the strategic acumen of our founder, Kaja Cheng. Her experience is a rare synthesis of elite business education and high-stakes corporate practice.

An alumna of the Saïd Business School, University of Oxford (EMBA), Kaja brings a framework of rigorous, analytical thinking to every engagement. This strategic foundation was honed during her tenure at PwC and Korn Ferry, where she served as the Head of Talent Acquisition for the ESG Business in Greater China and Consultant in Global Financial Markets. She navigated one of the world's most competitive talent markets, sourcing senior leaders for complex roles at the intersection of business, sustainability, and governance. This experience provides her and our clients with an insider’s understanding of the pressures and opportunities facing modern corporations.

The Human-Centric Approach: Coaching & Mentoring Excellence

Strategy alone is insufficient. Lasting impact is achieved when business objectives are aligned with human potential. Kaja's deep commitment to individual growth is codified in her professional credentials as an ICF Member and an IMC Accredited Mentor.

This is not just a passion; it is a discipline. She believes that true leadership development is a process of discovery, not just instruction.
